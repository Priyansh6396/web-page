About InAmigos Foundation

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO). It is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization, licensed by the Central Government. The foundation is based in Chhattisgarh and holds 80G, 12A, CSR-1 registration, NITI Aayog registration, and ISO 9001:2015 certification.

Every contribution is directed toward causes such as food distribution, education, women empowerment, animal welfare, environmental sustainability, and social inclusion. Join the change and help build a more inclusive, compassionate society.
